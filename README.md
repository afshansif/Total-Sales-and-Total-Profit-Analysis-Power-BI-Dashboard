# 📊 Total Sales and Total Profit Analysis — Power BI Dashboard

![Line Chart](line_chart.png)

---

## 📌 Overview

An interactive Power BI line chart visualizing **Total Sales** and **Total Profit** trends across a **3-year period (2022–2024)**, broken down by year and month. Built to help identify seasonal patterns, performance peaks, and low points in business revenue and profitability.

---

## 📈 What This Dashboard Shows

- **Dual-axis line chart** tracking Total Sales (left axis) and Total Profit (right axis) simultaneously
- **Month-by-month breakdown** across January 2022 to December 2024
- **Key statistical annotations** displayed directly on the chart:

| Metric | Sales | Profit |
|--------|-------|--------|
| **Maximum** | 463,354.08 | 108,698.28 |
| **Average** | 382,219.20 | 95,009.34 |
| **Minimum** | 310,213.79 | 80,663.96 |

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

- **Power BI Desktop** — report building and visualization
- **DAX** — calculated measures for Max, Min, and Average annotations
- **Data Modeling** — date hierarchy (Year → Month)
- **Dual Y-Axis** — to compare two metrics on different scales

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Sales and Profit follow a **similar trend pattern**, suggesting a strong correlation between the two
- A noticeable **spike around Oct–Nov 2022** marks the highest profit period in the dataset
- **Early 2022 and late 2024** record the lowest sales values
- Recurring **seasonal dips mid-year** are visible consistently across all three years
